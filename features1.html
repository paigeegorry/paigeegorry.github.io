Features

Learn more about all that EmployME can do for you.

First and foremost, we want you to look and feel your best! We have two consultants from GQ and Cosmopolitan, credited fashion sources, to help make sure you look your best. Your beauty is our priority. We also love to work with your style. Whether you like pant suits or dresses, double-breasted suits or tuxedos, we are here to make you look sharp!

Resume Templates

The ultimate goal is for you to attain employment and it is hard when you leave college and no longer have a Career Center at your fingertips. This is why we developed packages each month to provide you with resume templates and how-to guides on resume-building.

Interview Tricks

We congratulate all of our EmployME friends if they obtain an interview and we strive to provide the right interview tips and tricks so that you are the best applicant they have! Building confidence through interview preparation has been proven to increase employment possibilities.

Social Media Updates

We know that people love to update their social media post-grad to brag about all of the great things they're doing. It's so hard to keep up, so we provide you with successfully braggy social media updates across all platforms. Anything from travel journals to professional journeys, we're here to make your social media shine!

Words of Encouragement

It is discouraging to be unemployed after four (or more) long years of expensive schooling. We use unique software to match you with your perfect EmployME penpal to share frustrations, words of encouragement, and whatever you want to make this process easier.

Totally Free!

Here at EmployME, we are most excited to provide a service for you that is completely and totally free! We strive to find partners who will help pay for the services that you need, but cannot afford. We also save you time! Where you would once have to look to find all of these resources, we provide them to you in one helpful bundle!
