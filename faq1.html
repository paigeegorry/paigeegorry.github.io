Logistics

  • How much does an EmployME description cost?
  • How does EmployME get its funding?
  • What's the Catch?
An EmployME subscription is absolutely free! Yep, no charge at all. Just subscribe and tell your friends on social media. We gain our funds from anonymous doners, wealthy Wall Streeters, and Portland hipsters who had money before it was cool.
EmployME gets its funding through donations. Yep, rich EMPLOYED people have enough money to donate for the rest of us. We are very lucky to have an amazing team that are skilled at gaining donations from unsuspecting people. Check out our team to learn more about them!
There is no catch! We just want to provide you with an amazing product for free! Life should be a little more like this, we think.

Product

  • What kinds of boxes do you have?
  • How do I get my suit tailored?
  • Do I have to get everything in the box?
We have three options for giftboxes. One is geared toward an anti-consumerist market, the second is geared toward the modest middle class, and the third is for those who want to look and feel like a million bucks. Check out our products page.
We will schedule a time to get your clothing tailored in an email immediately when you sign up. We will provide you a time at your convenience to meet with a local tailor of your choosing. Again, it's free.
If you are not a fan of one of our items, please let us know with the form below! We are happy to accommodate you in anyway. We can even substitute chocolate for a healthier treat, if that is what you'd rather have.

Miscellaneous

  • How do I stop my subscription?
  • How do I donate to EmployME?
  • How do I contact you?
Unfortunately, you cannot stop your subscription until you die or get a job. If you get a job, we will be immediately notified by the PI we hired to follow you around.
EmployME doesn't ask for donations on our website because we think that is tacky. We will find you if you want to donate.
Check out our contact page! We would love for you to come to our HQ or just drop us a line by email.
